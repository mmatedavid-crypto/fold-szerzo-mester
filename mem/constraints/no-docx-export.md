---
name: No DOCX export
description: Word export of generated contracts is forbidden — IP / monetization risk
type: constraint
---
The user explicitly rejected DOCX export of the generated land lease contracts.

Reason: a downloadable .docx contains the editable template, which would let
users copy/redistribute the contract and never pay again. Only the rendered
PDF (with watermark / verification metadata) may be offered as a download.

Do not propose Word/DOCX export in any future feature plan.
